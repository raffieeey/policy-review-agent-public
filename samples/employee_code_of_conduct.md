# Employee Code of Conduct

## 1. Purpose
This code of conduct sets forth the standards of professional behavior expected of all employees. It promotes a positive work environment and ensures compliance with legal and ethical standards.

## 2. Professional Behavior
All employees are expected to:
- Treat colleagues with respect and dignity
- Maintain a professional demeanor in all business interactions
- Communicate openly and honestly
- Collaborate effectively with team members

## 3. Conflict of Interest
Employees must disclose any personal interests that could conflict with their professional responsibilities. Outside employment or business activities must be approved by management.

## 4. Confidentiality
Employees must protect confidential information obtained during their employment. This obligation continues after termination of employment.

## 5. Anti-Harassment
The organization maintains a zero-tolerance policy for harassment of any kind. All complaints will be investigated promptly and confidentially.

## 6. Gifts and Entertainment
Employees may not accept gifts or entertainment that could influence business decisions. Gifts valued over $50 must be reported to the compliance department.

## 7. Reporting Violations
Employees are encouraged to report violations of this code through the ethics hotline or directly to their supervisor. Retaliation against reporters is prohibited.

## 8. Disciplinary Action
Violations of this code may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment.
